About Pat's Salmon Cookies

Pat's Salmon Cookies are the salmon and cookie lovers dream! They combine finely ground premium NorthWest non-farmed, non-GMO, no-bad-stuff, and all the lables that we canuse to make us sound hip and inline with to make us work with the newest food and diet fads.

The famous Salmon cookie has been around since 1989 and took over the old but original Starbucks location to continue the trend of trendy coffee shops, but with an extra twist of tasty Salmon Cookies.

Have a cookie with your coffee, or have them almost with your salmo dinner to add a little sweet salmon to your life!
